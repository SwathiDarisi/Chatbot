About PSCMR College of Technology

Potti Sriramulu Chalavadi Mallikarjuna Rao College of Engineering and Technology (PSCMRCET) was started in the year 2008, under the aegis of, more than hundred years old, Sri Kanyaka Parameswari Vissamsetti Venkataratnam Hindu High Schools Committee (SKPVVHHSC – Established in 1906) , by a group of philanthropists belonging to Vijayawada. PSCMR College of Engineering and Technology offers B. Tech, MBA, M Tech and MCA programmes.
